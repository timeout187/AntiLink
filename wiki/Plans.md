# AntiLink Plans

AntiLink is sold in two independent ways. Server plans apply to a single
server. The AI Membership is separate, and is described further down this page.

## Server plans

A server plan applies to one server and is chosen by someone who manages that
server. Billing is monthly or yearly, and payments are handled by Paddle as
merchant of record.

### AntiLink Basic

Essential protection for free.

### AntiLink Plus

Full link protection, expanded whitelists, additional server controls, and
Community Health activity trends.

### AntiLink Premium

The full security suite for advanced server defense. AntiLink Premium is the
top tier and includes everything in AntiLink Plus, along with the Custom Bot
option for running AntiLink under your own bot identity.

Features and prices may change as AntiLink evolves. Use the
[official pricing page](https://antil.ink/premium) for the current comparison.

## AI Membership

AI Chat is not part of a server plan and is not unlocked by upgrading a server.
It runs on an **AI Membership**, which works differently:

- **User-owned.** The membership belongs to the person who buys it, not to a
  server. One person may hold more than one membership.
- **Token-metered.** Each tier includes a monthly pool of tokens that the
  assistant draws from. The pool resets at the start of each month.
- **Linked to one server.** A membership powers AI Chat in a single server at a
  time. The link can be moved to a different server later.

Memberships are offered in Silver, Gold, and Platinum tiers, monthly or yearly.
The tiers differ in the size of the monthly token pool, how long chat history is
kept, and whether general non-security conversation is available.

The AI assistant proposes; a person who holds the matching Discord permission
approves. Nothing is applied on its own.

See [[AI Membership]] for the tier breakdown. Current AI Membership prices are
on the [AntiLink AI page](https://antil.ink/ai).
